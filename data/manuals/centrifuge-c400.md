# CentraSpin C400 Centrifuge - User Manual

## Overview
The CentraSpin C400 is a high-speed clinical centrifuge designed for rapid sample processing. 

## Basic Operation
1. Connect device to a grounded clinic wall outlet.
2. Ensure the rotor is seated securely.
3. Place samples in a symmetrical layout. Maximum weight deviation across opposing tubes is 0.5g.
4. Close lid firmly until the latch clicks.
5. Set RPM and Timer, then press START.

## Error Codes
- **E-02 (Lid Open)**: The lid latch is not fully engaged. Open and press down firmly.
- **E-04 (Imbalance Error)**: The rotor weight is not distributed evenly. **WARNING**: Do not force the lid open if E-04 appears mid-cycle. The emergency brake is engaging. Power cycle the device and wait 60 seconds before opening.
- **E-12 (Motor Overheat)**: The internal motor temperature has exceeded safe limits. Unplug the device and request immediate biomedical service.

## Maintenance
- Wipe down the interior weekly with standard 70% isopropyl alcohol.
- Do not use bleach on the rotor, as it causes microscopic stress fractures.
