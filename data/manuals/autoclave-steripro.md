# SteriPro Autoclave - User Manual

## Overview
The SteriPro Autoclave uses high-pressure steam and heat to sterilize clinical instruments. 

## Daily Setup
1. Check the distilled water reservoir level. Refill if below the "MIN" line.
2. Load instruments only in approved sterilization pouches. Do not overpack the chamber.
3. Place a biological indicator stripe in the center of the load for the first cycle of the day.

## Cycle Failure
If a cycle fails to complete or the display shows a "Low Pressure" or "Heat-Seal Failure" warning:
- The instruments inside are **NOT STERILE**. 
- Abort the cycle.
- Do not attempt to use the instruments.
- Rerun a test cycle with an empty chamber and a new biological indicator. 
- If the test cycle fails, the door seal or heating element may be compromised. Lock the device out of service and escalate to Facilities.

## Warning
Never attempt to forcefully open the chamber door while the pressure gauge indicates > 0 PSI. Doing so can cause severe steam burns.
