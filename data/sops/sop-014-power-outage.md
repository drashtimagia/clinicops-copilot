# SOP-014 — Power Outage Response

## Purpose
Provide a standard clinic response to temporary or prolonged power disruption affecting medical devices, cold storage, or patient workflow.

## Trigger Conditions
Use this SOP when:
- power is lost in a room or department
- device restarts after outage
- refrigerated storage is affected
- connected equipment shuts down unexpectedly

## Immediate Actions
1. Confirm outage scope.
2. Identify critical equipment affected.
3. Prioritize patient safety and sample preservation.
4. Switch to backup power where available.

## Device Handling
### For monitors and pumps
- verify current power source
- confirm battery state
- move to backup device if needed

### For refrigerators
- keep doors closed
- monitor internal temperature
- move critical material if safe range is threatened

### For lab analyzers
- pause active runs
- verify calibration status after recovery
- restart only after stability is confirmed

## Communication
Notify:
- operations manager
- affected clinical staff
- biomedical technician if equipment fails to recover

## Recovery Checks
After power returns:
1. Restart only essential devices first.
2. Check alarms and fault codes.
3. Confirm time/date settings if required.
4. Validate device readiness before reuse.

## Escalation
Escalate if:
- power instability continues
- battery-backed devices fail to recover
- storage temperature exceeded safe threshold
- device calibration or function is uncertain

## Suggested broadcast
"Temporary power disruption affecting equipment availability. Please verify device readiness before use."
