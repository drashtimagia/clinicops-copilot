# VitalsPro M5 Monitor - User Manual

## Overview
The VitalsPro M5 is a mobile unit for tracking patient pulse, SpO2, and non-invasive blood pressure (NIBP).

## Power Management
The monitor operates on an internal Lithium-ion battery pack when not placed on the wall-mounted dock.
- **Dock Indicator Lights**: 
  - *Solid Blue*: Dock has power.
  - *Pulsing Green*: Monitor is actively charging.
  - *Solid Green*: Battery is charged and has passed health-check.
  - *Blinking Red*: Battery health failure. Battery must be replaced.

## Troubleshooting
- **Device shuts off mid-transport**: If the device was docked overnight but fails during transport, the battery may be reaching its end of life (typical lifespan is 18 months) or the dock pins may be damaged. Check the dock indicator lights. If health failure is indicated, replace the physical battery pack from local supply.
- **NIBP Cuff Inflation Failure**: If the blood pressure cuff fails to inflate, inspect the hose for kinks. If no kinks are found, run the internal pump recalibration via `Settings > Diagnostics > NIBP Pump Reset`.

## Cleaning
- Use approved hospital disinfectant wipes on the screen and chassis. Do not spray liquid directly onto the screen.
